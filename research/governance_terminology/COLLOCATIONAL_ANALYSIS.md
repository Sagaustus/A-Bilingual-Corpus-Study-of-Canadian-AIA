## Comparative Analysis

### Monitoring (EN) vs Contrôle (FR)

**EN "monitoring"** collocates suggest passive observation/tracking:
- system, outcomes, processes, continuous, performance

**FR "contrôle"** collocates suggest active management/verification:
- mesures, qualité, processus, vérification, gestion

**Interpretation:** English frames oversight as monitoring outcomes, while French frames it as active control/management.

---

### Accountability (EN) vs FR Alternatives

**EN "accountability"** appears with:
- institution, assigned, design, development, system

**FR "reddition de comptes"** would appear with administrative/reporting terms (insufficient data in sample_contexts)

**Note:** Limited FR accountability term data in sample contexts - full document analysis needed for robust collocational comparison.

---

### Bias (EN) vs Biais/Préjugé (FR)

**EN "bias"** (unified term) appears across technical and social contexts

**FR distinguishes:**
- **biais** — algorithmic/statistical contexts
- **préjugé** — social/ethical contexts

This lexical split reflects French documents' conceptual separation of technical vs social bias.

## Methodology

- **Window size:** ±5 words from target term
- **Stopword filtering:** Common function words removed
- **Source:** sample_contexts from 16 bilingual AIA pairs
- **Limitation:** Sample contexts may not capture all occurrences; full PDF analysis would yield more robust results
